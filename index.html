About

Hi! I am Eun-Young Ko, a Master's student in School of Computing at KAIST. I am advised by Juho Kim and a member of KIXLAB.

My research interest lies in Computer-supported Cooperative Work (CSCW) and Social Computing, which are subfields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).
