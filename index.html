About

Hi! I am Eun-Young Ko, a Ph.D. student in School of Computing at KAIST. I work with Juho Kim and am a member of KIXLAB and HCI@KAIST group.

My research interest is in human-computer interaction and social computing. I design and build interactive systems to improve discourse, collaboration, and understanding of information in online communities, crowdsourcing, civic engagement, and news consumption.

I recieved B.A. in Mathematics (with minor in Economics and Financial Engineering) from KAIST and then I studied Economics at Seoul National University for two years.

I served as a Committee Member of the Participatory Budgeting Program in Seoul for two years.

Stepping into the world of parenthood - on maternity and parental leave, celebrating and cherishing this special time until the year's end!
